Ingredients
3 fish
3 tsp mustard paste
1/2 green chilli paste
some black cumin seeds
1 tsp crashed coconut
as required mustard oil
Steps
Heat the pan,add mustard oil and add black cumin.
Fry for 1 minute and add mustard paste, green chilli paste,crushed coconut,salt,turmeric.
Then fry for 1 minute and add 1/2 cup water.
After 2 minutes add fish and sim the gas.
After 10 minutes switch off the gas and serve it with hot rice.
